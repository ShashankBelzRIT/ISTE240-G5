Accessibility

Accessibility ensures that everyone, regardless of physical or mental ability, can engage with digital experiences. It focuses on providing equal access and usability for people with diverse needs, such as visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive differences. This is not just a legal requirement in many regions — it’s a moral and design imperative that benefits all users.
