Fake News

Fake news has become something anyone with an online presence has come to fear ever since digital news began to build its mark on our world. Among the thousands of digital news sources out there, there are bound to be authors to misinform a reader to lead them to actions such as scams, identity theft, and fear of fake threats. Bias Breakdown

Bias in the news is a dangerous aspect of the news media. Political parties and individual beliefs have changed the face of news sources permanently. Countless news sources lean in certain ways away from the truth to sway readers a certain way towards the bias that the author follows. Bias has been an issue for centuries, before even the news media was present in society. Some ways to avoid bias in the news media you consume are finding a news source that you know produces reports that follow unbiased values.

In terms of the differences between print news and digital news sources for fake news and bias, here are some pros for both:

    Print
  • More likely to be written by a human
  • More reliable and trustworthy news
  • Generally less bias

    Digital
  • Easier to complete background research on the topic
  • Sources are easier to locate
  • Larger variety of sources
  • Further information on topics is more accessible
